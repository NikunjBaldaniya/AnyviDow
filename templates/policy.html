Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data.

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

AnyviDow is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Video URLs you submit for downloading
  • Download preferences and format selections
  • Basic usage analytics (anonymous)

How We Use Your Data

Processing Downloads

URLs are processed temporarily to fetch video information and generate download links.

Service Improvement

Anonymous usage data helps us improve performance and add new features.

Data Protection

Local Storage: Your download history is stored locally in your browser and never sent to our servers.
  • Downloaded files are automatically deleted from our servers after download
  • Temporary processing data is cleared within 24 hours
  • All connections are secured with HTTPS encryption

Third-Party Services

AnyviDow uses yt-dlp to process video information. No personal data is shared with video platforms beyond what's necessary to fetch public video metadata.

Your Rights

No Tracking
We don't track your browsing behavior
Data Deletion
Clear your browser data anytime

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
